About the practice

Therapy for people
who want to understand,
not just cope.

I'm Marcus Webb. I work with adults who sense that surface-level fixes aren't enough — people who want to understand why they feel the way they do, not just manage the symptoms.

My approach is depth-oriented: we look at patterns, history, the stories you've inherited, and what they cost you. The work is rigorous, but it's also warm, collaborative, and often surprising.

Areas of work

What brings people
to therapy.

01

Anxiety & Panic

The kind that lives in your body. Constant low-level dread, panic attacks, hypervigilance — we trace it to its roots.

02

Depression

Not just low mood, but the particular flatness that makes the future feel unreachable. Exploratory work with real depth.

03

Relationship Patterns

Repetition. Avoidance. Codependency. Understanding why your relationships play out the way they do — and changing it.

04

Trauma & PTSD

Including complex trauma and developmental trauma. Safe, paced work that doesn't rush or overwhelm.

05

Identity & Meaning

Who am I? What do I want? Existential questions that deserve real attention — not platitudes.

06

Grief & Loss

Bereavement, miscarriage, the loss of a life you expected. Space to grieve without a timeline.

07

Life Transitions

Career breakdown, divorce, burnout, becoming a parent — when the ground shifts beneath you.

08

Men's Wellbeing

A particular interest: working with men who've never felt able to talk. No agenda, no performance required.
